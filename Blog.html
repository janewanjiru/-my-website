Born a Crime By Trevor Noah

Summary Of The Book

The Book Born A Crime as it portrays itself the birth of Trevor Noah being
born by a white Swiss father and a black Xhosa mother during the apartheid
south Africa where blacks were not supposed to marry the whites.The Book
Born a Crime is the story of mischievious young boy who grows into a restless
young man as he struggles to find himself in a world where he was never supposed
to exist.Born a crime also a story of a young man's relationship with his fearless
rebellious,and fervently religious mother ,a woman determined to save her son from the cycle
of poverty,voilence,and abuse that would altemately threaten her own life.
